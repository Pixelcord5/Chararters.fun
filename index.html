Pomni

The Amazing Digital Circus

Pomni is the main protagonist of The Amazing Digital Circus, a 25-year-old woman who was trapped in a virtual reality circus after wearing a mysterious headset. Her name, meaning "remember" in Russian, was randomly assigned by the AI ringmaster Caine after Pomni forgot her real identity.

appearance and Physiology

She appears as a stylized jester with a large head, slim limbs, white skin, and a red-and-blue striped outfit with mismatched gloves and shoes. As a digital avatar, she possesses immortality, invulnerability, and an elastic body that can stretch and warp, though she retains human emotions and psychological trauma. Her eyes typically display a red and blue pinwheel pattern, which turns into chaotic scribbles when she is fearful or distressed. Personality and Role Initially, Pomni is highly anxious, paranoid, and fragile, struggling to accept her predicament and desperately seeking an escape. Despite her fear, she is empathetic and caring, often acting as a confidant for other trapped characters like Ragatha, Jax, and Gangle. Over time, she evolves from a panicked newcomer into a more grounded and resilient figure, showing courage and leadership while navigating the circus's psychological horrors. Background

Pomni's real name is Abagail Brooks

In the real world, she worked as a supermarket accountant and ran an urban exploration YouTube channel. Her digital form allows her to perform feats impossible for humans, such as surviving fatal falls and generating objects, but she cannot truly die; the only alternative is abstraction, where she loses her sense of self.
